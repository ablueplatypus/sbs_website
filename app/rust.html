Corrosion Protection

SBS has extensive experience providing high quality corrosion protection. Our system uses elastomeric coatings form a vapor and moisture barrier that prevents moisture and oxygen from penetrating the coating, which prevents further rust and growth. This system is revolutionary and is a maintainable system for life and will save any company or building owner thousands if not millions of dollars over the life of the steel. We look forward to servicing your corrosion protection needs.
